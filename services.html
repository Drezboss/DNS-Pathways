Our Services

DNA Pathways CIC offers three core service pathways designed to provide comprehensive support for families in Gloucestershire. Each pathway can be accessed independently or combined for holistic family support.

Institutional Advocacy

Navigate complex systems with expert support. We help families understand their rights and access the services they need.

  • EHCP applications and reviews
  • Benefits applications and appeals
  • School admissions and disputes
  • Tribunal representation
  • Discrimination support
Learn More About Advocacy

Social Care Pathway

Professional guidance through social care processes with wraparound family support during difficult times.

  • Safeguarding support and navigation
  • Family crisis intervention
  • Care planning and reviews
  • Multi-agency meeting support
  • Family preservation strategies
Learn More About Social Care

Football & Family Pathway

Inclusive sport sessions for children with SEND, combined with family support and community building.

  • Weekly inclusive football sessions
  • Family activity programs
  • Peer support networks
  • Holiday programs
  • Community events
Learn More About Football

How Our Pathways Work Together

While each pathway offers specialized support, our approach recognizes that families often need multifaceted assistance. A family might start with institutional advocacy for an EHCP, discover social care needs during the process, and benefit from the community connection offered through our football pathway.

Coordinated Support: When families access multiple pathways, we ensure all support is coordinated and complementary, avoiding duplication and maximizing impact.
