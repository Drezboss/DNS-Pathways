Institutional Advocacy

Navigate complex systems with expert support. Our institutional advocacy service helps families understand their rights, access appropriate services, and ensures their voices are heard when dealing with institutions.

EHCP Support

  • Initial EHCP applications
  • Annual reviews and amendments
  • Appeals and tribunal representation
  • School placement disputes
  • Transition planning (14+)

Benefits Advocacy

  • DLA/PIP applications and appeals
  • Universal Credit support
  • Carer's Allowance guidance
  • ESA assessments
  • Tribunal representation

Education Advocacy

  • School admissions appeals
  • Exclusion challenges
  • SEN support disputes
  • Transport appeals
  • Reasonable adjustments

Discrimination Support

  • Disability discrimination cases
  • Reasonable adjustments requests
  • Equality Act 2010 guidance
  • Complaint procedures
  • Legal referrals when needed

Our Approach

We believe that effective advocacy combines:

  • Lived Experience: Our team includes advocates who have navigated these systems personally
  • Professional Expertise: Deep knowledge of legislation, procedures, and best practices
  • Family Partnership: We work with you, not for you - building your confidence and skills
  • Persistent Support: We don't give up when faced with initial refusals or delays
  • Holistic View: Understanding how different systems interact and affect your family

What to Expect

  1. Initial Consultation: Free discussion to understand your situation and goals
  2. Strategy Planning: Together, we'll map out the best approach for your case
  3. Evidence Gathering: Help collecting and organizing supporting documentation
  4. Representation: Accompanying you to meetings, hearings, or tribunals
  5. Follow-up: Ensuring decisions are implemented and monitoring progress

When to Contact Us

Consider institutional advocacy if you're experiencing:

  • Delays or refusals in EHCP assessments or provision
  • Benefits applications being rejected or reduced
  • School refusing reasonable adjustments
  • Exclusions or threats of exclusion
  • Feeling overwhelmed by complex processes
  • Previous unsuccessful appeals you want to challenge
  • Discrimination based on disability

Success Stories

Our advocacy has helped families achieve:

  • Successful EHCP applications for children previously denied assessments
  • Increased benefit awards following successful appeals
  • School placements in specialist provisions
  • Overturned exclusions and improved school relationships
  • Compensation for discrimination
  • Better understanding of rights and increased confidence
