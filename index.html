About DNA Pathways

Standing beside families in crisis

Our Mission

To stand beside families in crisis — with expert, compassionate support that breaks through red tape and builds real outcomes.

Who We Are

We're a husband-and-wife team with over 30 years' combined experience in social care, safeguarding, and institutional advocacy. We're also parents to a disabled child. We understand what it means to feel invisible, unheard, or blamed by the very systems meant to help.

What Makes Us Different

  • We combine professional insight with lived experience
  • We work with families, not just for them
  • We blend casework, coaching, and community in one integrated model

Our Services

Three powerful support pathways. Access one, two, or all three — depending on what you need.

Social Care Pathway

For families who need help navigating services, safeguarding, or care assessments.

  • Care Act & Children Act navigation
  • Safeguarding advocacy
  • Social worker meeting prep
  • Crisis coordination

"We were finally listened to — and things started moving fast."

Book Social Care Support

Institutional Advocacy Pathway

For education, benefits, housing, or employment system support.

  • EHCP applications & appeals
  • Benefits (PIP, DLA, UC) advocacy
  • Discrimination & complaints escalation
  • Tribunal preparation & documentation

"We won our appeal because of them. I can finally breathe."

Get Advocacy Help

Football & Family Pathway

For children with SEND to build confidence and community through inclusive sport.

  • Inclusive football sessions
  • Peer play for siblings
  • Family drop-in support
  • School holiday clubs
