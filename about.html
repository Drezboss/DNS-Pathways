About DNA Pathways CIC

Our Mission

DNA Pathways CIC exists to provide comprehensive support for families navigating the complex landscape of social care, SEND services, and advocacy needs in Gloucestershire. We combine professional expertise with lived experience to deliver services that truly understand and meet families' needs.

What Makes Us Different

  • Lived Experience Leadership: Our team includes professionals who have personal experience of the systems we help families navigate
  • Holistic Approach: We don't just address immediate crises â€“ we build long-term family resilience
  • Three Pathway Model: Our unique approach combines advocacy, social care support, and inclusive activities
  • Local Expertise: Deep knowledge of Gloucestershire services, schools, and support networks
  • Family-Centered: Every intervention is designed around the whole family's needs, not just the individual

Our Expertise

Our team brings together professionals with backgrounds in:

  • Social work and safeguarding
  • SEND advocacy and EHCP navigation
  • Education and inclusive sports
  • Benefits and tribunal representation
  • Family support and crisis intervention

Our Values

  • Empowerment: We help families develop the skills and confidence to advocate for themselves
  • Inclusion: Every family deserves support, regardless of their circumstances
  • Integrity: We operate with transparency, honesty, and ethical practice
  • Innovation: We constantly evolve our approaches based on families' feedback and emerging best practice
  • Partnership: We work collaboratively with families, professionals, and communities

Community Impact

Since our inception, DNA Pathways CIC has been committed to making a real difference in Gloucestershire communities. We measure our success not just in cases resolved, but in families thriving, children included, and communities strengthened.
