Get Support

Urgent Support: If you're in immediate crisis or have safeguarding concerns, please contact emergency services (999) or your local authority's emergency duty team.

Getting support from DNA Pathways CIC is straightforward. We accept referrals from families, professionals, and self-referrals. Choose the method that works best for you.

What Happens Next?

  1. Initial Contact: We'll contact you within 24 hours to discuss your needs
  2. Assessment: We'll arrange a convenient time to understand your situation better
  3. Support Plan: Together, we'll create a plan that matches your family's needs
  4. Action: We'll start providing support through the appropriate pathway(s)

Our Commitment to You

  • All initial consultations are free
  • We work with your existing support network
  • Everything is confidential unless safeguarding concerns arise
  • You remain in control of your support journey
  • We'll never judge - we're here to help
